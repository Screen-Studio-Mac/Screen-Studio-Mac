## Overview

Screen Studio Professional Suite is a complete cinematic screen recording platform that serves professionals and creators who need their software demonstrations and tutorial recordings to look as polished as their products.

Export quality options produce recordings at the resolution and frame rate appropriate for each destination, from compressed web delivery through high-resolution files suitable for professional video production and client presentation.

Replay and trim controls allow recordings to be reviewed and shortened immediately after capture, keeping the production workflow efficient without the overhead of a full video editing application for basic trim and review operations.

Multiple app window modes allow recording of a single application window in isolation from the rest of the desktop, producing clean demonstrations focused entirely on the software being shown without desktop background or other application interference.

Screen Studio Professional Suite is the screen recording platform for professionals who believe that the quality of how their software is demonstrated reflects on the quality of the software itself, and who want recordings that represent their work at the same standard as every other aspect of their product communication.

## Performance & Integration

Screen Studio is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Screen Studio processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
